The Blue Billy Kay

The Blue Billy Kay has Water blast and Cloudy mist as his super powers. He wears a Bubble Cape with a splash symbol on it. He can create hi own Hurricane to destroy the army submarines and also he becomes more powerfull when it rains.

Navigation to the lair

  1. start from RBI castle to through coin den
  2. Then you'll reach gold garden after travelling for 2 hours on Dollar drive
  3. There you need to get into Aren with Axis Lord and take a fight
  4. After winning the fight you can get on the Muthoot maharath and it will take you to Midwestt fort
  5. Then from Midwest fort we need to go through River of Rascals and finally you'll reach your destiny
